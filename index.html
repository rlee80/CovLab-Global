About Us

CovLab Global
Collaborate on COVID-19 solutions

Unite to fight Corona

One of our goals is to help people from around the world connect and make a solution to fight back against Covid-19 by making solutions.

Win prizes for learning

Another goal is to make sure that contestants get rewarded for their hardwork by giving them rewards provided by our Sponsors who made this all possible!

Connect with others

Another important goal is to make sure that people interact with other, meeting new people and exploring each others skills makes these days at home a learning experience.
